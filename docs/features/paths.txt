Paths:
- Paths are what can be considered in terms of RPGs as "Paths/Jobs/Classes/etc"
    - Paths are what people invest their "Path Points" into as they level-up.
    - Paths can unlock:
        - Skills
        - Feats
        - Path Rewards
        - Etc...
    - Certain Paths/Path Levels can also be required to unlocks other Paths.
        - Examples:
            - Warrior 5 + Mage 5          -> Unlocks Battle-Mage Path
            - Warrior 5 + Pyromancer 5    -> Unlocks Dragon-Warrior Path
            - Mage 5    + Necromancer 5   -> Unlocks Lich Path
