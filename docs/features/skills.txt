Skills:
- Skills are Passive/Active Feats that can be leveled.
    - Defining Characteristics:
        - Has Levels
            - Grants either Active/Passive Bonuses or Unlocks new Feats.
            - Grants Path-Points at certain points.
        - Has Experience
            - You gain experience from doing "Something"
    - By leveling up your Skills you eventually unlocks more Path Points.
    - By leveling these they either provide some kind of:
        - Passive Bonus
        - Active Bonus
        - Unlocks other Feats
    - Gaining Experience for Skills can be done in different ways depending on what's setup for the skill.
        - Mining Certain Blocks
        - Killing Mobs
        - Casting Spells (addon)
        - Completing Multiblocks (addon)
